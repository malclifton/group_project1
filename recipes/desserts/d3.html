Rasberry Galette

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Ingredients

  • 1 pie crust, store-bought or homemade
  • 2 cups raspberries
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon turbinado sugar
  • 1 large egg, beaten

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat; set aside.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine raspberries, sugar and cornstarch.
  3. Roll out pie crust and transfer to prepared baking sheet. Spread raspberry mixture evenly over the top, leaving a 1-inch border. Sprinkle with turbinado sugar.
  4. Fold the border over the edge of the raspberries, covering the filling by about 1 inch. Brush the edge with beaten egg.
  5. Place into oven and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
  6. Let cool 5 minutes before serving.
